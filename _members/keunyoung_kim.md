Keunyoung Kim received a B.S. degree in Electronic Engineering from Kyung Hee University in August 2025. Motivated by a strong interest in computer architecture, he joined GAUDI Lab as a research intern. His research interests focus on memory-system bottlenecks and CXL-enabled heterogeneous systems. He aims to study CXL from a system-architecture perspective and explore architectural techniques. Through active engagement in the lab, he is eager to deepen his expertise and contribute to next-generation high-performance, energy-efficient computing systems.
