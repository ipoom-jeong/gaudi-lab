Seonghun Jeong is a Master's student in the Department of System Semiconductor Engineering at Yonsei University. As a researcher at GAUDI Lab, his research focuses on optimizing GPU memory systems to overcome performance bottlenecks in modern computing. He is particularly interested in efficiently utilizing Unified Virtual Memory and accelerating address translation in GPU architectures. His goal is to design innovative architectural solutions for high-performance computing systems.
